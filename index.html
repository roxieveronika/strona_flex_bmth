Band

Members

The current lineup features a balanced mix of original founders and newer energy, each bringing a distinct musical identity. Their vocalist shifts seamlessly between guttural lows and soaring cleans, while the guitarists build intricate layers of melody and aggression. Anchored by a tight rhythm section, the band’s chemistry onstage is as intense as their studio recordings.

Listen on Spotify

History

Forged in the dim corners of basement venues, the band came together out of a shared obsession with crushing breakdowns and melodic dissonance. Over the years, their sound evolved from raw, chaotic riffs into a more atmospheric and dynamic form of metalcore. Despite lineup changes and sleepless touring cycles, their commitment to pushing the genre’s boundaries has never wavered.

Listen on Spotify

Collabs

Throughout their career, they’ve teamed up with producers, vocalists, and instrumentalists from across the heavy music scene. These collaborations often introduce unexpected elements—ambient textures, electronic pulses, or guest vocal hooks—that expand their sonic palette. Each partnership pushes them into fresh territory without compromising their core sound.
